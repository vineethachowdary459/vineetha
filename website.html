QIS COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY



Qis College of Engineering & Technology (QISCET,QISIT,B.PHARMACY) is a centre of excellence in industry learning and has set up to develop human resource assets to cater to the petroleum and allied energy sectors, improve knowledge base of policy makers and technologists and provide a competitive edge to leaders to compete in the global arena.
